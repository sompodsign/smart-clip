FAQ

Common questions.

Is my clipboard data stored in the cloud? ⌄

No. SmartClip stores everything locally in a SQLite database on your Mac. Your clipboard data never leaves your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no telemetry.

What macOS versions are supported? ⌄

SmartClip requires macOS 12 Monterey or later. It is a native Mac app built with Tauri and Rust, so it runs efficiently on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs.

How do I activate my Pro license? ⌄

After purchase, you receive a license key by email. Open SmartClip, click "Activate Key" in the footer, enter your key, and you're done. Pro features activate instantly.

Can I use it on multiple Macs? ⌄

Each Pro license allows activation on one Mac at a time. You can deactivate and reactivate on a different machine whenever you need.

What is your refund policy? ⌄

If SmartClip doesn't work for you, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund — no questions asked. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime.

Does SmartClip slow down my Mac? ⌄

SmartClip is built with Rust and is extremely lightweight — under 5 MB installed. It uses virtually no CPU when idle and minimal memory. You won't notice it's running.
