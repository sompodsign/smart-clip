# Product Facts — SmartClip

## What It Is
SmartClip is a macOS clipboard manager that silently captures everything you copy and makes your full clipboard history instantly searchable, pinnable, and re-pasteable.

## How It Works
1. SmartClip runs in the background as a lightweight process
2. Every time you copy text or an image, SmartClip saves it automatically
3. Open SmartClip to see your full clipboard history with search and filters
4. Click any item to restore it to your clipboard — paste it anywhere immediately

## Key Features
- Clipboard history: captures all text and images you copy
- Instant search: filter your entire history in real time
- Pin items: keep important snippets (API keys, addresses, templates) pinned to the top
- App source tracking: records which app each item was copied from
- Text & image support: screenshots, code, URLs, prose — all captured
- One-click restore: click to put any item back on your clipboard
- Fully local & private: SQLite database on your Mac, no cloud, no accounts, no telemetry
- Lightweight: under 5 MB, built with Tauri + Rust, near-zero CPU/RAM when idle

## Requirements
- macOS 12 (Monterey) or later
- Works on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs
- No internet connection required
